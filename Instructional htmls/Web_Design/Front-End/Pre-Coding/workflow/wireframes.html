Wireframes

  • These are simple drawings outlining the position and size of items in your website.
  • They are the first step in User Experience design, and web design in general, and they enable you to create a low-quality version of how your website will look.
  • You can use any tool for this, even a pen and paper, but professional tools exist out there which can enable you to design the site for different screen sizes and give you more accuracy in-terms of Spacing and sizes of elements in your website.
  • Figma is one of such tools. It is free for most part, and you can make very good mock-ups of your website with it.
  • You can use other tools such as Adobe Photoshop or Illustrator; but those tools weren't designed for web designers, so they lack many functionalities that Figma has. So learn Figma.
