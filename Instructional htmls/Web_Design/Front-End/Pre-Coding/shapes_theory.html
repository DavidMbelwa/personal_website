Shapes communicate certain feelings to the audience. Below are some of popular shapes in designing and their perceived emotion.

Circles

  • Positivity
  • Community, Friendship, Love
  • Perfection, Attention Seeking

Oval Shapes

  • Sturdiness, Endurance, Stability

Squares / Rectangles

  • Power and Strength
  • Professionalism, Efficiency, Stability
  • Balance, Practicality
  • Perfection Moderninsm

Straight Vertical Lines

  • Strength, Masculinity, Energy

Horizontal Lİnes

  • Calmness, Balance, Tranquility
  • Secure, Settled

Angled Lines

  • Movement, speed
  • Energy, dynamic movement

Websites whose content usually contain these kind of themes usually adopt an approach with a lot of squares though. This is to say that if you have these themes in your design list, don't hesitate to start with squares and rectangles and then experiment with angled lines later in your design.

Curved Lines

  • Femininity, happiness, generosity, sense of rythm
  • Innovation (when used with angular lines)
