Introduction

I'm preparing these notes as a personal interest on molecular biology continues to develop since few days ago (approximately 24th of July, 2023) when I first decided to look up what are functional proteins. Since then I've developed interest relating to this field and this interest up to now (29th July, 2023) has led me to revisit introduction to Biology and Chemistry.

These notes are an attempt to organize my understanding of these topics, and build a stable foundation on them. When I started this journey of exploration, I kept bumping into concepts that I didn't understand, and this has compelled me to start from the beginning. The arrangement is from General Chemistry to Organic Chemistry and then to molecular biology.

Enjoy!
